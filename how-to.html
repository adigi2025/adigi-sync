How To use Adigi Sync

Privacy-first, in-browser conversion. No uploads.

  1. Open the tool (live site or open index.html locally).
  2. Load a file: drag & drop CSV / XLSX / JSON or click Browse.
  3. Options: Target format, CSV delimiter (, or ;), Header, Normalise, Deduplicate.
  4. Preview appears on the right. Then press Convert & Download.

Troubleshooting

  • CSV delimiters vary by locale. Try ; if commas look wrong.
  • Very large spreadsheets need free RAM (browser only; nothing uploads).
